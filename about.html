Beyond the Browser

My journey, side quests, and all the fun little things in between.

High energy individual with an active lifestyle, fueled by passions like road cycling, gym training, hiking and yoga.

After earning my postgraduate business degree in 2009, I kicked off my career with an internship at a renowned Microsoft Development Agency. Immersed among top tech talent, I played a key role in rebranding the company and orchestrated a successful SEO site migration - a challenge that ignited my passion for organic search.

That passion led me to Signs Express, the UK’s leading sign maker, where I handled my first large-scale website project. Through relentless testing and strategic determination, I significantly boosted their organic search visibility and laid the groundwork for the proven processes I still use today.

After several years working in the UK’s SEO trenches, I took a break to travel - a decision that unexpectedly fuelled my career further. While backpacking through Southeast Asia and working remotely, I expanded both my professional expertise and personal perspective. In 2013, I joined Web Marketing Experts in Melbourne, an experience that deepened my understanding of digital strategy and business.

In 2014, I advanced my career in Sydney with Omnicom/Resolution Media, managing large international client accounts, and then moved to New Zealand to work with Starcom/Publicis Groupe. Splitting my time between Auckland and Wellington, I honed my technical SEO and digital strategy skills on major projects.

Eventually, I relocated to Brisbane and embraced full-time freelance work, serving as a white-label SEO resource for multiple agencies. This phase broadened my portfolio across Australia, the UK, and New Zealand.

Just before Covid, I returned to the UK and collaborated with high-profile clients through consulting roles focused on technical SEO strategy and on-page optimisation, solidifying my reputation as a seasoned SEO contractor.

Now, after nearly a decade of international freelance work, I am ready for the next big leap. I am eager to bring my rich experience, global perspective, and passion for SEO back into an exciting new role. With a vision to grow with a forward-thinking international company and a personal dream of starting a family, I am excited to take on new challenges, where my skills can contribute to lasting success.
